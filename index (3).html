Our Story

Where tradition meets
every single stitch

Born in the lush hills of Nagaland, Granny Crochet is more than a brand — it's a legacy of patience, love, and artistry passed down through generations. Each piece is handcrafted with care, blending traditional Naga heritage with contemporary design.

From delicate bouquets that never wilt to charming keychains that carry warmth in your pocket, every creation tells a story of skilled hands and a devoted heart. Our founder, a State-recognised Master Craft Person, pours decades of expertise into each stitch.
